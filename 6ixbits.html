About 6ixBits

6ixBits is a virtual, student-led hackathon open for high-school and university students, predicated on both helping to network inspired, like-minded individuals as well as encouraging hackers to express their innate creativity and capability for innovation.

Revolving around issues of global significance, 6ixBits aims to take both beginning and skillful coders alike on 3-day long experience that facilitates learning, growth, and networking opportunities. Most of all, we hope that 6ixBits hackers gain the courage and vision to tackle global issues in their own unique ways.

6ixBits, despite being a virtual event, is also designed to immerse hackers through workshops, keynotes, game nights and other virtual activities to provide a relaxed and memorable experience for all.

The executive members of TYN are ecstatic about 6ixBits; we hope it to be an unforgettable three days for all hackers!

ABOUT THE ORGANIZING COMMITTE

College of Engineering, Kidangoor (CEK) is one among the premier institutions in the state. The college is governed by the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education established by the Government of Kerala. The institution glistens with outstanding records in both academic and extracurricular fields backed by excellent faculty and full-fledged facilities The whole Event is the collabration of all the Technical Clubs and Societies of the Institute. This includes the The Open Source Club, TinkerHub CEK, FOSS Club, Infin CEK, Coding Club, Mozilla Campus Club and Developer Students Club. These Clubs themselves hold 80% of all the Technical Activities in the Institute and has played a vital role in developing the Students with technical Skills and helps increase their Job Prospects and Future.
